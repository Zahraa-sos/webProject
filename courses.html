Circle

A circle is a shape that is perfectly round,
like a wheel or a pizza.

Triangles

A triangle is a shape with three sides and corners.
It looks like a piece of pizza with three straight sides

Fractions

Fractions are numbers that show parts of a whole
and they are made up of two numbers.

Functions

A function is like a magical machine
that takes a number, does something to it,
and gives you back a new number.

Equations

An equation is like a math puzzle
that uses numbers and symbols to make a sentence
that says two things are the same.

Special Parallelograms

A parallelogram is a special kind of four-sided
shape, or quadrilateral, where opposite sides
are equal in length and parallel
